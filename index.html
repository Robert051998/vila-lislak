About Vila Lisjak

Villa Marana is an elegant holiday home located in Brela, a famous touristic place on Makarska Riviera. It is situated in a quiet part of Brela just at the beach and on the 8 km of Longo Mare, a coastal walk of the town, where you can enjoy the most beautiful and interesting landscape.

This wonderful beachfront Villa is ultra-modern designed and is unlike any other home you'll find in the Makarska Riviera. It offers full privacy with stunning views on the island Brač and Hvar and Biokovo mountain.

Top 10 world’s most beautiful beaches

By Forbes Magazine

In July 2004 The American magazine Forbes included Brela’s Punta Rata Beach in the top ten most beautiful beaches in the world. It is the most beautiful clear water beach in Croatia already awarded by many prestigious international awards. In 2014 The Huffington Post website described Brela as dream destination and a perfect reason to get on a plane to Croatia as soon as possible.

According to Belgian website European Best Destinations – Punta Rata is one of the most beautiful beaches in 2015. On the Western side of the beach there is the Brela Stone – a symbol of Brela, the most common motive from postcards of Brela and Croatia. The rock is covered in pines proudly protruding and presenting all its beauty and force, power and spite to grow from stone.

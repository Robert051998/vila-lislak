Privacy Policy

Privacy policy and General data protection regulation (GDPR)

Vila Lisjak operates in accordance with General data protection regulation. GDPR provides a framework for setting a high standard of personal data protection of all EU citizens. Clients and partners with whom VILA LISJAK communicates or cooperates, are subject to all necessary standards and business practices that provide personal data protection. Personal data is any data related to an individual whose identity has been identified or can be identified.

Agreement for data collection

Personal data are collected in a transparent and legitimate way. All data collected must be under clear and precise agreement collected. Every subject needs explicitly allow its data to be stored and used for a clearly defined purpose. It must also be clearly defined why the data is collected and how they will be processed.

Cookies

Our site collects “Cookies” . Cookies makes for you an easier and better user experience In compliance with the European regulation, we have adhered to the policy of privacy and the use of cookies. We use cookies to enable for you to use our online services, to improve our user experience and functionality of our web sites. By using our website, you agree to use cookies. Embedded content from other websites behaves in exactly the same way as if the visitor had visited another website. Data storage

We process and store data only as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which the data was collected. You can ask Vila Lisjak for an insight into the method of storage, use and distribution of your personal data, and the deadline for delivery of the request is 15 days from the date of submission of the request. Right to delete

The client can request the deletion of data, and the deletion is carried out at his request, except in cases where the data is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which the data was collected. After deletion, the client receives a confirmation of the deletion of personal data.

Users and partners whose data is processed by Vila Lisjak have the right to know the purpose for which their data is processed and the period in which the data will be processed.
